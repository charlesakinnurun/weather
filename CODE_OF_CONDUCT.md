# Code of Conduct

[![Code of Conduct](https://img.shields.io/badge/Code%20of%20Conduct-Open‑Source-blue.svg?style=for-the-badge)](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces in the **BlackStone Stock** project — including code, documentation, issue trackers, mailing lists, chat channels, wikis, blogs, social media, and any other communication channels used by the project.

---

## Our Community Pledge

We as contributors, maintainers, and bystanders pledge to make participation in this project a harassment‑free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We commit to being:

- **Inclusive:** Welcoming and supportive to all, using inclusive language.  
- **Welcoming:** Ensuring a safe space where anyone feels they can join and contribute.  
- **Respectful:** Listening to differing viewpoints, giving and receiving feedback constructively, and collaborating toward decisions that grow the project.  
- **Collaborative:** Sharing work clearly, documenting changes, and keeping communication open.

---

## Our Responsibilities

Everyone participating in this project — contributors, maintainers, and others — has a responsibility to uphold this Code of Conduct at all times. Unacceptable behaviors include (but are not limited to):

- Violent threats, abusive or discriminatory language.  
- Insults or demeaning comments related to gender, identity, sexual orientation, disability, mental health, race, religion, politics, or other characteristics.  
- Posting sexually explicit or violent content.  
- Unwelcome sexualized language or advances.  
- Harassment in any form, public or private.  
- Sharing private information (e.g. personal addresses) without consent.  
- Any behavior that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.  
- Encouraging or promoting any of the above behaviors.

---

## Enforcement & Reporting

If you observe or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, you can report it by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed as needed. Responses may include:

- A private response to the reporter  
- Public or private reprimands, warning, or temporary or permanent ban  
- Community discussion or mediation  

All reports will be handled with discretion and care.
